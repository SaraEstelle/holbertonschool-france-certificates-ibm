IBM Certificates – Trimester 1
Holberton School France – IBM Partnership

📘 Overview
This repository contains the certificates completed as part of the IBM Certificates – Trimester 1 project from the Holberton School France curriculum.
The project leverages the Holberton x IBM partnership, giving students access to IBM’s e‑learning platforms and enabling them to earn industry‑recognized digital credentials.

The trimester includes two mandatory certificates:

Introduction to Open Source

Agile Explorer – Powered by Agile at IBM

Both courses are introductory and require no prior knowledge.
Content and requirements are based on the official project page .

🌐 Platform Clarification
Holberton France students use two distinct IBM learning platforms:

✔️ SkillsBuild.org
Officially covered by the Holberton x IBM partnership

Hosts the mandatory certification courses

✔️ SkillsNetwork
Not covered by the partnership

Contains additional recommended learning content

Requires a separate account

➡️ You must create an account on both platforms if you use both.

🧑‍💻 Access & Login Requirements
To avoid login or access issues:

Log in using your @holbertonstudents.com email address

Always use “Log in with Email” on both platforms before opening course links

Ensure your first and last name are correct, as they appear on your certificates

When asked to specify your learner type, select “apprenants adultes” to prevent access problems

These requirements are explicitly stated in the project instructions .

📝 Evaluation Process
Certificates must be uploaded to the correct folder with the correct filename

If the certificate provides a shareable link, include it in the repository

Your SWE (Software Engineering Evaluator) will manually verify and score your project

You must notify your local SWE once your certificates are uploaded

These evaluation rules come directly from the project page .
